How to multi-select items?

  • Hold down Ctrl key. Click the items one by one.
    OR     
  • Select an item at first. Hold down Shift key, click another item, all the items between these two items will be selected.

How to drag and drop?

Click the selected items, hold down the mouse left button and drag the cursor.
